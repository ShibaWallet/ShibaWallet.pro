Frequently Asked Questions

How many blockchains does ShibaWallet support?

ShibaWallet now supports Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, HECO, OKEx Chain, Polygon, Fantom Chain, Klaytn Chain, Gate Chain, Celo Chain, Optimism Ethereum, Arbitrum One, Hoo Smart Chain, and Avalanche C-Chain, Moonbeam, Moonriver, and Moonbase Alpha, Aurora, Boba Network, tens of blockchains etc., and it'll soon be supporting Solana and NEAR chain.

Can I create my own wallet on ShibaWallet app?

Yes, you do can! Create your new address through the button on top left corner, in an opened page, you'll see `Create a new wallet` button that fixed in the bottom.

Can I import my existent wallet into ShibaWallet app?

Yes, you do can! Import your existent wallet through the button on top left corner, in an opened page, you'll see `Import using Secret Recovery Phrase` button that fixed in the bottom.

How can I recover my wallet by seed phrase?

Easy to do that. As mentioned in above question `Can I import my existent wallet into ShibaWallet app?`, you just enter or paste your seed phrase or Mnemonics or Secret Recovery Phrase in the following page after you click the button `Import using Secret Recovery Phrase`.
